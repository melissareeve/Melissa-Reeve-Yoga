Our Vision

Created from our love, curiosity and passion for the journey inwards, Melissa Reeve yoga draws from the timeless principles of the Sutras and has its roots in Vedic practice. While deeply rooted in the traditional wisdom of the ancient yogis, classes are infused with a modern understanding of the human body and consciousness.

Classes are designed to transform you into a knowledgeable and confident practitioner and build a stronger connection to yourself and the world around you.

Teaching Philosophy

Yoga for me is a spiritual, expressive, mindful, and caring practice. It can help to bring me into my body and helps me be present with discomfort which ultimately makes me more resilient and flexible within daily life.

As a teacher I am keen to help other people cultivate their own connection to the asana and develop their awareness. It is my aim to help them be mindful and facilitate them to link the body and mind to the breath, to the asana, and to the moment.

I have taught Corporate classes, at City Fitness, Abundance studio Wellington, in high schools, at the Bhakti Lounge and at the Te Aro Astanga centre.
