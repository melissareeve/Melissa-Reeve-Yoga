Melissa Reeve Yoga

Created from our love, curiosity and passion for the journey inwards, Melissa Reeve yoga and meditation draws from the timeless principles of the Sutras and has its roots in Vedic practice. While deeply rooted in the traditional wisdom of the ancient yogis, classes are infused with a modern understanding of the human body and consciousness.

Classes are designed to transform you into a knowledgeable and confident practitioner and build a stronger connection to yourself and the world around you.
